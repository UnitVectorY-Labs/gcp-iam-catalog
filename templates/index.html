Welcome to gcp-iam-catalog

gcp-iam-catalog is an independent tool designed to simplify the exploration of Google Cloud Platform (GCP) IAM roles and permissions. This tool is not affiliated with or endorsed by Google.

Why Use gcp-iam-catalog?

Managing IAM roles and permissions in GCP can be challenging due to fragmented documentation. gcp-iam-catalog addresses these challenges by providing:

  • Role-to-Permission Mapping: Easily view the permissions included in each IAM role across all GCP services.
  • Permission-to-Role Mapping: Identify all roles that grant a specific permission, aiding in implementing least privilege access.

Get Started

Explore the Roles to see their permissions or visit the Permissions section to find roles that include specific permissions. Enhance your GCP access management with ease.
